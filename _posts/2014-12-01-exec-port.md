 PORT Returns the port of the connection.

 Usage: port=pgmex('port',dbConn)

 INPUT:
   regular:
     dbConn: uint32 or uint64 [1,1] - C pointer to a PGconn structure
 OUTPUT:
   regular:
     port: char [1,N] - connection port
